“The last sword” - warrior story

He had nothing left. No army.
No name. 😢 Just an old sword and a promise.

The village was burning.
People were running. But he stood in the middle of the road.
"Not today," he whispered.

He didn’t fight for a king.
He fought because someone had to.
That night,
20 families slept safely because one man chose to stay.

*Moral*: A warrior is not the one with the biggest sword.
A warrior is the one who stands when everyone else sits down

Fatima and The Honest Truth" ⚖️❤️

" Fatima 16 saal ki thi. Uska sapna tha *lawyer* banna. Wo kehti thi: _"Main kamzor logon ki awaaz banungi."_ Ek din school me 1000 rupay gum ho gaye. Sab ne class monitor Ali par shak kiya. Principal ne kaha: _"Jo sach batayega use inam milega."_ Fatima ne dekha tha... paise galti se uski dost Ayesha se gir gaye the. Ayesha darr gayi thi aur chup ho gayi thi. Dusre din assembly thi. Sab ki nazar Fatima par thi kyunki wo sabse honest maani jati thi. Principal ne poocha: _"Fatima, tumhe kuch pata hai?"_ Fatima ka dil zor se dhadak raha tha. Agar wo sach bolegi to uski best friend ko saza hogi. Agar jhooth bolegi to ek begunah Ali ko ilzam milega. Usne gehri saans li aur bola: _"Sir, paise Ayesha se gir gaye the. Usne darr ke maare nahi bataya."_ Class me sannata. Principal ne Ayesha ko maaf kiya aur Fatima se kaha: _"Tum jesi imandari hi ek ache lawyer ki pehli quality hai."_ Us din Fatima samajh gayi. *Lawyer banna talwar chalana nahi, sach ka saath dena hai.* Tabhi se Fatima roz kanoon ki kitab ke sath imandari bhi padhti hai 📚
*Moral:* Sach kadwa hota hai, par wo jeet dilata hai.
